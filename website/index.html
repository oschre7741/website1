Ode to Moth

Oh, moth that loves the light;
Who would go through heaven and hell for it,
Perilous and long may be your flight,
And hard it might be to find a way to fit,
Yet you surely will find a way,
To make it through to your love,
To discover the path to your prize.
As they always say,
You and the light are like in winter a hand and glove,
And she is the apple of your eyes.
